Resident Evil 4 Remake

Platform - PC

Price : $60

Take a long journey to the New World, following in the footsteps of the Elder Dragons. Can you discover what prompts them to migrate there every decade in the event known as the Elder Crossing?

story

It is six years after the horrific events in Raccoon City. The president's daughter disappears without a trace, and it seems she stays in Spain in the hands of a mysterious organization. The only person able to save her is Leon S. Kennedy - a former cop working for the US government. To accomplish this challenging task, the man travels to an isolated village inhabited by hostile locals and members of a secret cult.

Gameplay

RE4 Remake is the classic gameplay mechanics from 2005, enhanced with improved controls and more dynamic scenes. Direct the actions of an ex-cop to unravel the mystery of a strange Spanish village. It's a forgotten, gloomy place featuring highly realistic and unsettling sceneries that will send shivers down your spine. Explore, collect equipment and solve puzzles to discover a way out of this desperate situation.
